Projects

Scholarship Matcher

A system that provides suggestions to students seeking scholarship opportunities for higher studies across a variety of countries. The application takes into account various factors such as academic performance, financial need, field+ level of study, and personal background to match students with suitable scholarships. Students can create profiles, input their information, and receive personalized scholarship recommendations based on their unique circumstances. Tech stack used: HTML, CSS, Javascript for the frontend, Flask for the backend, Supabase for the database and Hugging Face models for the intelligent matching. Further enhancements are in the pipeline to include features such as application tracking, deadline reminders, and resources for scholarship applications. Find the hosted version here.
